# LinkedIn Post - English

Local AI is powerful, but debugging the stack is still painful.

Your app says it cannot connect. But is the problem CUDA? NVIDIA drivers? Ollama? vLLM? SGLang? Dify? Open WebUI? Docker? A wrong `/v1` endpoint?

InferDoctor is an open-source local AI stack doctor and setup assistant.

It gives you:

- one-command health check
- Top Fixes
- capacity preview
- stack recommendations
- starter templates
- template validation
- dry-run setup plans

The goal is not to download models or run inference for you. The goal is to help you understand what is broken and what to do next.

GitHub: https://github.com/anguoyang/inferdoctor
